EOW Concept

Under EOW concept the dealership provides door step service to customers. It can be for regular maintenance service or for break down. The dealership must have a vehicle branded as per company guidelines and equipped with tools & equipment and one technician specially identified & trained for this job. EOW technician shall visit the customers place to render the services.

Points to note

Dealers

1. Expert on Wheels is a new initiative started by TVSM to attract the irregular customers, by sending EOW technician to the customer preferred location.

2. We strongly recommend only Scheduled Service Maintenance is to be done under this program and all repair job should be done at the Service Centre

3. No additional charge will be charged for the Service job, but a nominal charge for convenience will be deducted.
Convenience Charges: Rs 50 (if customer within 5km), Rs 75(if within 10km)

4. Payment is collected as cash payment onsite against invoice submitted.

5. It is mandatory to operate within the dealer working hours, but it is dealer’s suitability to operate before or after the operating hours.

6. Working on public holidays is not a mandatory option. Again it is dealer’s suitability and market requirement.

7. We strongly suggest to plan the service properly and arrange the parts accordingly before the EOW technician leave for the service. In case of such incident is unavoidable, it cannot be charged and it should be done on goodwill basis.

8. No fixed models are planned as transportation for expert on wheels, but preferably Max 4R or Scooty Streak can be used for this purpose.

9. A basic training will be given by TVSM AO or Area Training Centres for this program.

10. No discount is to be offered if the technician does not reach on time. the technician should update the customer incase of any delay due to unavoidable circumstances.

Customers

1. Expert on Wheels is a new initiative started by TVSM to attend the busy or away customers, by sending EOW technician to the customer preferred location.

2. Call to the Dealership and take appointment for the EOW service at least one day prior to the required service date.

3. We strongly recommend only Scheduled Service Maintenance is to be done under this program and all repair job should be done at the Service Centre.

4. No additional charge will be charged for the Service job, but a nominal charge for convenience will be deducted.
Convenience Charges: Rs 50 (if customer within 5km), Rs 75(if within 10km)

5. Payment can be done as cash payment onsite against invoice submitted by the EOW technician.

6. The operating hours are as per the Dealer suitability and operating hours

7. Working on public holidays is again as per dealer suitability.

8. No cost will be charged for normal Scheduled Service requested for travel time to go and get the parts, but incase part is required as per your suggestion at the site, additional charge will be deducted

9. We sternly believe in First Time Right, therefore no repeat jobs should arise. Incase of exceptional cases the job will be done on goodwill basis.

10. The EOW technician will be trained by professional trainers from TVSM.

11. The EOW technician will report at your location based on the appointment time given to you. Incase of any delay due to unavoidable circumstances it will be informed by the Service Advisor.

12. As per TVSM protocol the customer site after service should be cleaned thoroughly by the EOW technician.
