Insured Declared Value Schedule

Insured Declared Value is the maximum Sum Assured (compensation) fixed by the insurer which is provided on theft or total loss of vehicle. Refer below schedule for IDV rates.

Age of the vehicle

% of Depreciation

Not exceeding 6 months

5%

Exceeding 6 months but not exceeding 1 year

15%

Exceeding 1 year but not exceeding 2 years

20%

Exceeding 3 years but not exceeding 4 years

30%

Exceeding 1 year but not exceeding 2 years

40%

Exceeding 4 years but not exceeding 5 years

50%

Note: IDV for the vehicle of age exceeding 5 years would be determined mutually by insurer and insured
